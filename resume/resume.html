About
Hi, I'm Victoria Stavish!

I am a second year student at the University of Maryland studying multiplatform journalism and information science. I have experience in print, digital and audio reporting, HTML and CSS, Rstudio, Python and Excel.

This summer, I am doing a data and computational journalism internship at The Howard Center for Investigative Journalism where I will be analyzing police records. I also work at The Diamondback, The University of Maryland's indpendant, student-run paper, where I cover science and technology as a staff writer.

I have experience in audio reporting thanks to my time covering health at WMUC, UMD's student-run radio station. I have also served as a copyeditor for Stories Beneath The Shell, another student-run publication.

In the past, I have also worked as a social media and engagement editor for small businesses and non-profits. There, I learned to analyze customer data and donor data, SEO techniques and developed standard operating procedures for both internal and external communications

I'm looking for positions where I can report on, analyze, visualize and write about data. I am especially interested in bringing data journalism resources to small newsrooms, as I'm from a rural town in Ohio with little access to local news.

When I'm not working, you can usually find me lifting weights, doing yoga, playing sports or hanging out with my cat, Miss Pants. I'm an avid non-fiction reader (but am trying to get into more fiction!) and I also enjoy cooking, knitting and wearing big earrings.
